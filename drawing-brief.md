# Drawing Brief

## Required Views

- Family overview plate showing `NAF-6H`, `PVC-TRAIN`, `LEGACY-SW`, and `SONGBOOK` as separate lanes.
- NAF body longitudinal section with bore, slow-air chamber, true sound hole, nest, and bird block relationship.
- Top view with six finger-hole stations measured from the foot end.
- Bird/fetish underside channel detail with flue depth, flue width, and air-path datum.
- PVC proof-flute section after stock measurement.
- Legacy SolidWorks normalization plate once the selected archive model is recovered.

## Authority Boundary

The current `drawings/flutes-family-layout.svg` and `drawings/flutes-family-layout.dxf` are scaffold plates. They document the family split and packet routing. They do not contain cut-ready hole stations, bore dimensions, or toolpaths.

Fabrication drawings must be generated from extracted workbook rows or reviewed CAD, then registered in `visual-output-register.csv`.
